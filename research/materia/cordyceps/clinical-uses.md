# Cordyceps - Clinical Uses

## Renal Dysfunction

### Meta-Analysis Evidence (Wu 2025)
**Study Design**: 15 RCTs, 1,310 participants
**Source**: [Frontiers in Medicine](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/39839641/)

**Diagnostic Performance**:
- Pooled sensitivity: 0.89 [95% CI: 0.84-0.93]
- Pooled specificity: 0.69 [95% CI: 0.59-0.77]
- Diagnostic odds ratio: 18.0 [95% CI: 8.0-39.0]
- Area under curve: 0.88 [95% CI: 0.85-0.90]
- Positive likelihood ratio: 2.8 [95% CI: 2.1-3.9]
- Negative likelihood ratio: 0.16 [95% CI: 0.10-0.27]

**Clinical Improvements**:
- Significantly reduced blood creatinine levels
- Shortened oliguria period
- Increased urine osmolality
- Improved creatinine clearance rate
- Reduced blood urea nitrogen (BUN)

**Preparations Used**:
- Bailing Capsule
- Jinshuibao
- Zhiling formulations

**Dosing**: 3-6 g/day for chronic renal failure

**Safety**: No adverse events or complications reported

## Hemodialysis Support

### Meta-Analysis Evidence (Ong 2019)
**Study Design**: 12 trials, 655 participants
**Source**: [Journal of Pharmacy and Pharmaceutical Sciences](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/32186018/)

**Specific Effect Sizes**:
- **C-reactive protein reduction**: Standardized mean difference -0.61
- **High-sensitivity CRP**: Reduced by 3.44 mg/L
- **Serum albumin**: Increased by 3.07 g/L
- **Malondialdehyde**: Reduced by 1.95 nmol/L (antioxidant effect)
- **Hemoglobin**: Increased by 9.56 g/L

**No Significant Effects On**:
- Serum creatinine
- LDL cholesterol

**Evidence Quality**: Insufficient due to limited trial numbers and unclear methodological quality

**Safety**: No explicit side effects or complications mentioned

## Lung Cancer Adjuvant Therapy

### Meta-Analysis Evidence (Wang 2024)
**Study Design**: 12 RCTs, 928 participants
**Source**: [Phytomedicine](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/38484953/)

**Tumor Response**:
- Relative risk: 1.17 [95% CI: 1.05-1.29]
- Interpretation: 17% improvement in tumor response rate

**Immune Function Improvements**:
- **CD4+ T cells**: Mean difference +4.98 percentage points [95% CI: 1.49-8.47]
- **NK cells**: Mean difference +4.17 percentage points [95% CI: 2.26-6.08]
- **IgG**: Mean difference +3.95 g/L [95% CI: 0.98-6.92]

**Quality of Life**:
- **Karnofsky Performance Status**: Mean difference +8.20 [95% CI: 6.87-9.53]

**Chemotherapy Side Effect Reductions**:
- **Myelosuppression**: RR 0.38 [95% CI: 0.19-0.75] (62% reduction)
- **Leukopenia**: RR 0.76 [95% CI: 0.63-0.92] (24% reduction)
- **Radiation pneumonitis**: RR 0.74 [95% CI: 0.62-0.88] (26% reduction)

**Optimal Dosing**: 6 g/day for 21 days per cycle, 3-4 cycles

## COPD (Chronic Obstructive Pulmonary Disease)

### Meta-Analysis Evidence (Yu 2019)
**Study Design**: 15 trials, 1,238 participants (GOLD stages 2-3)
**Source**: [Evidence-Based Complementary and Alternative Medicine](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/31073318/)

**Improvements Demonstrated**:
- **FEV1% predicted**: Significant improvements in forced expiratory volume
- **6-minute walking distance (6MWD)**: Enhanced exercise endurance
- **St. George's Respiratory Questionnaire (SGRQ)**: Improved quality of life scores
- **Respiratory symptoms**: Significant improvements

**Safety**: No serious adverse events reported

**Evidence Limitations**: No placebo-controlled studies of high methodological quality

## Long COVID

### Randomized Controlled Trial (2025)
**Study Design**: Waitlist-controlled, 110 participants (55 treatment, 55 control)
**Source**: [Acta Materia Medica](https://www.scienceopen.com/hosted-document?doi=10.15212/AMM-2024-0089)

**Intervention**: Cs4 extract for 12 weeks

**Primary Outcome - Long COVID Symptoms**:
- Mean difference: -10.1 [95% CI: -14.1 to -6.1], p<0.001
- Scale: Modified COVID-19 Yorkshire Rehabilitation Scale

**Fatigue Improvement**:
- Mean difference: -8.1 [95% CI: -14.2 to -2.0], p=0.011
- Scale: Brief Fatigue Inventory Form

**Additional Improvements**:
- Insomnia (Insomnia Severity Index)
- Respiratory symptoms (St. George's Respiratory Questionnaire)
- Anxiety and depression (Hospital Anxiety and Depression Scale)
- Quality of life (Short Form 12 health survey)

**Safety**: No severe adverse events related to interventions

## Exercise Performance in Elderly

### RCT: Cs-4 Exercise Study (Chen 2010)
**Study Design**: Double-blind, placebo-controlled, 20 participants (ages 50-75)
**Source**: [Journal of Alternative and Complementary Medicine](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/20804368/)

**Intervention**: Cs-4 (CordyMax) 333 mg capsules, 3x daily (1,000 mg/day total) for 12 weeks

**Metabolic Threshold**:
- Increase: 10.5%, p<0.02
- Baseline: 0.83 ± 0.06 L/min
- Post-treatment: 0.93 ± 0.08 L/min

**Ventilatory Threshold**:
- Increase: 8.5%, p=0.031
- Baseline: 1.25 ± 0.11 L/min
- Post-treatment: 1.36 ± 0.15 L/min

**VO2 Max**: No changes in either group

**Placebo Group**: No significant changes

**Safety**: No adverse events reported

**Results**:

## Immune Function

### RCT: C. militaris Beverage (Ontawong 2024)
**Study Design**: Randomized, placebo-controlled, 20 participants (10 males, 10 females)
**Source**: [Scientific Reports](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/38580687/)

**Intervention**: Fermented Cordyceps militaris functional beverage containing 2.85 mg cordycepin per serving for 8 weeks

**NK Cell Activity**:
- **Males**: Significant increase from baseline to 4 weeks (p=0.049)
- **Females**: Significant increase at 8 weeks versus placebo (p=0.023)

**Anti-inflammatory Effects**:
- **IL-1β in males**: Decreased (p=0.049)
- **IL-6 in females**: Decreased from baseline (p=0.047)

**Safety**: No liver, kidney, or blood component toxicity; blood sugar and lipid indices showed no differences between groups

**Conclusion**: FCM can potentially be developed as immune-boosting supplement without toxicity

## Clinical Trial Dose Range Summary

- **Minimum effective dose**: 1 g/day Cs-4 (exercise performance)
- **Standard dose range**: 3-4.5 g/day (general use)
- **Higher dose range**: 6 g/day (lung cancer, severe renal conditions)
- **Cordycepin-specific**: 2.85 mg/day (immune support, C. militaris)

## Chinese Pharmacopoeia Traditional Uses (Since 1964)

**Official Status**: Recognized as "Dong Chong Xia Cao" (冬虫夏草) since 1964
**Source**: [NCBI Bookshelf](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK92758/)

**Traditional Properties**:
- Taste: Sweet
- Nature: Neutral
- Meridians: Lung and Kidney

**Traditional Actions**:
- Replenish kidney (补肾)
- Soothe lung (润肺)
- Stop bleeding (止血)
- Eliminate phlegm (化痰)

**Traditional Clinical Applications**:
- Fatigue and weakness
- Chronic cough
- Hyposexuality
- Asthenia after severe illness
- Renal dysfunction and renal failure

**Traditional Dosing**: 3-9 g crude drug as decoction
