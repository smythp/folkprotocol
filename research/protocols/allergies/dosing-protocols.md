# Dosing Protocols for Allergy Treatment

## Acute vs Chronic Allergies: Dosing Strategies

### Acute Seasonal Allergies (Hay Fever, Pollen Allergies)

**Definition**: Symptoms triggered by seasonal allergens (pollen, ragweed, etc.), typically lasting weeks to months during allergy season.

**Dosing Strategy**: Shorter-duration, symptom-focused protocols

---

#### For Acute Sneezing/Nasal Symptoms

**Black Cumin (Nigella sativa)** - BEST FOR ACUTE
- **Dose**: 250-500 mg seed oil 1-2x daily
- **Duration**: 15-30 days for seasonal allergies
- **Onset**: Within 15-30 days
- **Rationale**: Shorter trial period appropriate for seasonal use

---

**Butterbur** - RAPID ONSET
- **Product**: Ze 339 extract (PA-free only)
- **Dose**: 1 tablet 4x daily (8mg total petasin)
- **Duration**: 2 weeks minimum
- **Onset**: Effects seen within 2 weeks
- **Comparison**: Equivalent to cetirizine in 2-week trial
- **Rationale**: Fastest-acting herbal option

---

#### For Acute Eye Symptoms

**Perilla (rosmarinic acid extract)**
- **Dose**: 200 mg daily (optimal; 50-200mg range studied)
- **Duration**: 3 weeks trial documented
- **Onset**: Within 3 weeks
- **Safety**: Zero adverse events

---

**Quercetin**
- **Dose**: 200 mg daily (for allergic rhinitis)
- **Duration**: 4 weeks minimum
- **Onset**: Within 4 weeks
- **Alternative**: 500-1000 mg daily divided doses for general allergies

---

#### Acute Seasonal Protocol

**2-Week Rapid Response**:
- Butterbur Ze 339 (PA-free): 1 tablet 4x daily
- Expected: Results within 2 weeks, comparable to antihistamines

**4-Week Seasonal Protocol**:
- Quercetin 200mg daily + Perilla 200mg daily (for eye symptoms)
- OR Black Cumin 250-500mg oil 1-2x daily
- Expected: Noticeable improvement within 3-4 weeks

---

### Chronic/Perennial Allergies (Year-Round)

**Definition**: Ongoing allergic rhinitis triggered by dust mites, pet dander, mold, etc. Symptoms present most of the year.

**Dosing Strategy**: Longer-duration protocols targeting immune modulation and comprehensive symptom control

---

#### For Chronic Comprehensive Symptoms

**Tinospora cordifolia** - BEST FOR CHRONIC
- **Dose**: 300 mg aqueous stem extract 3x daily (900 mg total)
- **Standardization**: >5% bitter principles
- **Duration**: 8 weeks for full effect
- **Expected timeline**:
  - Improvements begin within 4 weeks
  - Full effect (61-83% complete relief rates) by 8 weeks
- **Rationale**: Immune modulation requires time; sustained relief justifies longer trial

---

**Astragalus** - IMMUNE REBALANCING
- **Dose**: 80 mg mineral complex containing A. membranaceus root extract
- **Standardization**: >5% bitter principles
- **Duration**: 6 weeks
- **Expected timeline**: Effects within 6 weeks
- **Best for**: Chronic runny nose
- **Rationale**: Modulates Th1/Th2 balance, requires time for immune rebalancing

---

**Reishi** - LONG-TERM CONGESTION
- **Dose**: 2g extract twice daily (4g total/day)
- **Duration**: 8 weeks
- **Expected timeline**: Dose-dependent effects within 8 weeks
- **Best for**: Chronic nasal blockage
- **Rationale**: Sustained immune modulation for persistent congestion

---

#### Chronic Perennial Protocol

**8-Week Comprehensive Protocol**:
- Tinospora 300mg 3x daily (comprehensive relief)
- OR Cure-Allergic-Rhinitis Syrup (CS) - TCM formula
- Expected: Gradual improvement over 4-8 weeks, sustained benefits beyond treatment period

**Alternative 6-Week Protocol**:
- Astragalus 80mg complex daily (especially for runny nose)
- Reishi 2g twice daily (especially for congestion)
- Expected: Effects within 6-8 weeks

---

### Preventive Dosing (Pre-Season)

**Strategy**: Start before allergy season to build immune resilience

**Yu-ping-feng San (Jade Windscreen)**
- **Traditional use**: Prevention, immune strengthening
- **Timing**: Begin 4-8 weeks before expected allergy season
- **Duration**: Continue through season
- **Rationale**: "Windscreen" barrier against allergens; builds Wei Qi (defensive energy)

**Stinging Nettle** - FREEZE-DRIED
- **Dose**: 300 mg twice daily (600 mg/day total)
- **Timing**: Begin 1-2 weeks before expected symptoms
- **Duration**: Continue through season
- **Rationale**: Patient satisfaction high (48% rated equal/better than previous medications)

---

## Detailed Dosing by Herb

### Butterbur (Petasites hybridus)

**Critical Safety**: ONLY use PA-free (pyrrolizidine alkaloid-free) extracts

**Standard Dosing**:
- **Product**: Ze 339 extract
- **Dose**: 1 tablet 4x daily (8mg total petasin per day)
- **Alternative**: 50 mg twice daily

**Duration**:
- **Minimum**: 2 weeks
- **Acute seasonal**: 2-4 weeks
- **Chronic**: Up to 8 weeks (maximum studied safety duration)

**Timeline**:
- **Onset**: Within 2 weeks
- **Peak effect**: 2 weeks (head-to-head trial duration)

**Clinical Evidence**: 2-week trial showed equivalence to cetirizine

---

### Stinging Nettle (Urtica dioica)

**Root Extract**:
- **Dose**: 150 mg daily

**Freeze-Dried Leaf**:
- **Dose**: 300 mg twice daily (600 mg/day total)

**Duration**:
- Acute: 2-4 weeks
- Chronic: Up to 8 weeks

**Timeline**: Effects within 2-4 weeks

**Note**: 58% of patients found it effective; 48% rated equal/better than conventional medications

---

### Perilla (Perilla frutescens)

**Rosmarinic Acid Extract**:
- **Dose Range**: 50-200 mg daily
- **Optimal Dose**: 200 mg daily (better efficacy in trial)

**Duration**:
- **Trial length**: 3 weeks
- **Recommended**: 3-4 weeks minimum for acute
- **Chronic**: Up to 8 weeks

**Timeline**:
- **Onset**: Within 3 weeks
- **Best for**: Eye symptoms (itchy/watery eyes)

**Safety**: Zero adverse events, normal blood tests

---

### Quercetin

**Allergic Rhinitis**:
- **Dose**: 200 mg daily

**General Allergies**:
- **Dose**: 500-1000 mg daily, divided doses

**Animal Studies** (for reference):
- **Dose**: 10-25 mg/kg

**Duration**:
- **Minimum**: 4 weeks
- **Study length**: 4 weeks in 66-participant RCT

**Timeline**:
- **Onset**: Within 4 weeks
- **Benefits**: Eye itching, sneezing, nasal discharge, sleep disturbances

**Special Note**: Bioavailability can be low - consider lecithin formulations for better absorption

---

### Tinospora cordifolia (Guduchi/Giloy)

**Standard Dosing**:
- **Dose**: 300 mg aqueous stem extract 3x daily (900 mg/day total)
- **Standardization**: >5% bitter principles

**Duration**:
- **Study length**: 8 weeks
- **Recommended minimum**: 8 weeks for full effect

**Timeline**:
- **Improvements begin**: Within 4 weeks
- **Peak/full effect**: 8 weeks
- **Complete relief rates at 8 weeks**:
  - Sneezing: 83%
  - Nasal discharge: 69%
  - Nasal obstruction: 61%
  - Nasal pruritus: 71%

**Safety**:
- Mild effects: 2/36 nasal pain, 1/36 headache (resolved with analgesics)
- No discontinuations required

**Best For**: Chronic allergies requiring comprehensive symptom relief

---

### Black Cumin (Nigella sativa)

**Seed Oil Capsules**:
- **Dose**: 250-500 mg oil 1-2x daily

**Calculated from Animal Studies**:
- **Animal dose**: 40-80 mg/kg/day
- **Human equivalent** (70kg adult): 2.8-5.6g
- **Practical clinical dose**: 250-500mg oil (concentrated extract)

**Duration**:
- **Seasonal allergies**: 15-30 days
- **Safety study**: 250 mg/day for 2 weeks (no adverse effects)

**Timeline**:
- **Meta-analysis**: Effects seen within 15-30 days
- **Improvements**:
  - Nasal symptoms: 41.5% (vs 13.4% placebo)
  - Ocular symptoms: 53.1% (vs 26.3% placebo)

**Comparison**: As effective as mometasone furoate (steroid nasal spray) in animal studies

---

### Astragalus (Astragalus membranaceus)

**Mineral Complex**:
- **Dose**: 80 mg containing A. membranaceus root extract
- **Standardization**: >5% bitter principles

**Duration**:
- **Study length**: 6 weeks
- **Recommended**: 6 weeks minimum

**Timeline**:
- **Onset**: Within 6 weeks
- **Strongest effect**: Rhinorrhea (runny nose), p=0.048

**Additional Benefits**:
- Improved total symptom scores
- Improved quality of life

**Best For**: Chronic runny nose

---

### Reishi (Ganoderma lucidum)

**Extract Dosing**:
- **Dose**: 2g extract twice daily (4g total/day)

**Duration**:
- **Animal studies**: 8 weeks
- **Human safety study**: 10 days (2g twice daily, no adverse effects)
- **Recommended**: 8 weeks for therapeutic effect

**Timeline**:
- **Expected**: Dose-dependent effects within 8 weeks
- **Best for**: Nasal blockage/congestion (early and late phase)

**Safety**: No adverse effects vs placebo in human trial (16 participants, 10 days)

---

### Spirulina (Arthrospira platensis)

**Standard Dose**:
- **Dose**: 2g daily

**Duration**: Not specified in head-to-head trial, likely 4-8 weeks

**Timeline**:
- **IL-4 reduction**: 32% decrease observed
- **Comparison**: May be superior to cetirizine

**Evidence Quality**: Limited but strong RCT data

---

### Curcumin (from Turmeric)

**Important**: Single curcumin showed NO significant effect on TNSS in comparative trial

**Recommendation**: Use in combination (e.g., Ashwagandha + Curcumin complex)
- **Combination TNSS reduction**: 31.62%
- **Single curcumin TNSS reduction**: 0%

**Implication**: Curcumin for allergies requires synergistic partner

## Dosing Decision Algorithm

### Step 1: Classify Allergy Type

**Acute Seasonal**:
→ Shorter protocols (2-4 weeks)
→ Focus on rapid-acting herbs (Butterbur, Black Cumin, Perilla)

**Chronic/Perennial**:
→ Longer protocols (6-8 weeks)
→ Focus on immune modulators (Tinospora, Astragalus, Reishi)

---

### Step 2: Select Based on Primary Symptom + Timeline Needs

**Need Relief in 2 Weeks**:
- Butterbur Ze 339 (PA-free) 4x daily

**Need Relief in 3-4 Weeks**:
- Perilla 200mg daily (eyes)
- Quercetin 200mg daily (eyes, sneezing)
- Black Cumin 250-500mg oil 1-2x daily (comprehensive)

**Can Wait 6-8 Weeks for Best Results**:
- Tinospora 300mg 3x daily (comprehensive, 61-83% complete relief)
- Astragalus 80mg for 6 weeks (runny nose)
- Reishi 2g twice daily for 8 weeks (congestion)

---

### Step 3: Consider Prevention

**Pre-Season (4-8 Weeks Before)**:
- Yu-ping-feng San (Jade Windscreen)
- Stinging Nettle 300mg twice daily (begin 1-2 weeks before)

---

### Step 4: Adjust Based on Response

**If Inadequate Response at Minimum Duration**:
1. Continue for full duration (e.g., 4 weeks if only tried 2 weeks)
2. Increase dose (if within studied range)
3. Switch to formula (combination formulas outperform single herbs)
4. Add symptom-specific second herb

**If Good Response**:
- Continue for full studied duration
- For seasonal: May discontinue after season
- For chronic: Consider sustained use up to 8 weeks (maximum studied safety)

---

## Safety-Based Duration Limits

### Maximum Studied Durations (Safety Data)

**8 Weeks Maximum**:
- Most herbs studied up to 8 weeks
- "Well-tolerated up to 8 weeks" (systematic review, 5,085 participants)
- Common side effects comparable to placebo

**Specific Safety Studies**:
- Tinospora: 8 weeks (minimal adverse events)
- Reishi: 8 weeks animal studies, 10 days human safety
- Butterbur: Up to 8 weeks studied (PA-free only)
- Black Cumin: 2 weeks documented (250mg/day), likely safe longer
- Quercetin: 4 weeks studied

**Long-Term Use (>8 Weeks)**:
- Limited safety data
- Consult healthcare provider for extended use
- Monitor for any adverse effects

---

## Combination Dosing Guidelines

### Safe to Combine

**Symptom-Specific Pairing**:
- Perilla 200mg (eyes) + Astragalus 80mg (runny nose)
- Butterbur 4x daily (congestion) + Quercetin 200mg (eyes)

**Formula + Single Herb for Dominant Symptom**:
- CS formula + Perilla 200mg (if severe eye symptoms)
- YS formula + Butterbur 4x daily (if severe congestion)

---

### Consult Healthcare Provider Before Combining

- Multiple formulas simultaneously
- Herbs + pharmaceutical medications (antihistamines, steroids)
- Long-term combinations (>8 weeks)
- High doses of multiple herbs
