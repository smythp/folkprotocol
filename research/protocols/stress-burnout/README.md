## Key Research Findings

### 1. No Head-to-Head Trials Exist
**Source:** bibliographies/stress-burnout-protocol.yaml:591

All comparative statements (Herb A > Herb B) are based on **separate trials vs. placebo**, not direct herb-vs-herb RCTs. This is the most important limitation to understand.

---

### 2. The Ashwagandha vs. Rhodiola Distinction Is Key
**Source:** bibliographies/stress-burnout-protocol.yaml:152-160

**Ashwagandha:**
- Direct cortisol reduction
- Calming, sleep-promoting
- Best for anxiety-dominant stress

**Rhodiola:**
- Cortisol response modulation
- Energizing, performance-enhancing
- Best for fatigue-dominant stress

**Together:**
- Complementary (calm + focus)
- "Steady, calm focus under stress"

---

### 3. Timeline Expectations Matter
**General rule:** 4-12 weeks for full HPA axis benefits (bibliographies/stress-burnout-protocol.yaml:508-512)

**Exceptions:**
- **Ashwagandha:** Days to weeks for calming (subjective), 56-60 days for cortisol (objective)
- **Bacopa:** 1 dose for acute stress reactivity
- **TCM formulas:** 2+ months for vitality changes

---

### 4. Symptom-Specific Selection Outperforms Generic "Adaptogen"
Matching herb energetics to symptom pattern produces better outcomes than generic "stress support."

**Clinical approach:**
- Anxiety + sleep issues → Ashwagandha (first-line)
- Mental fatigue + performance → Rhodiola (first-line)
- Both anxiety + fatigue → Holy Basil OR Rhodiola + Ashwagandha
- Chronic fatigue syndrome → Eleuthero (strongest CFS evidence)

---

### 5. TCM Formulas Have RCT Evidence
**Not just traditional use:**
- **Bu-Zhong-Yi-Qi-Tang:** 89% improvement in CFS RCT (bibliographies/stress-burnout-protocol.yaml:408-412)
- **Sijunzi Decoction:** Randomized, double-blind, placebo-controlled, multi-center trial (2024) (bibliographies/stress-burnout-protocol.yaml:414-422)
- **Xiao Yao San:** Widely used for burnout, traditional evidence

---

### 6. Licorice Is a Special Case
**Different mechanism:** Cortisol-sparing (inhibits breakdown) vs. HPA normalization

**Indications:**
- Low cortisol states
- Mild adrenal insufficiency
- **NOT for general stress**

**Safety:**
- Professional supervision required
- Can cause high BP, electrolyte issues (bibliographies/stress-burnout-protocol.yaml:576-580)

## Evidence Quality Summary

**Strongest Evidence (Grade A/A+):**
- Ashwagandha (cortisol, anxiety, stress)
- Rhodiola (mental performance, burnout)
- Holy Basil (HPA dampening, balanced effects)

**Good Evidence (Grade B/B+):**
- Panax Ginseng (HPA stabilization)
- Schisandra (HPA balancing, BDNF)
- Eleuthero (chronic fatigue)
- Bacopa (acute stress, cognition)
- Reishi (neurasthenia, sleep)

**Promising/Traditional (Grade C+/Traditional):**
- Licorice (special mechanism, limited human data)
- TCM formulas (traditional use + some RCTs)

## Research Gaps and Limitations

**From bibliographies/stress-burnout-protocol.yaml:589-596:**

1. **No direct head-to-head trials** between major adaptogens
2. **Variability in study designs** limits comparability
3. **Schisandra:** Most research in animals, human trials needed
4. **TCM formulas:** Need better diagnostic standards and methodology
5. **Combination ratios:** Optimal multi-herb formulas not established
6. **Long-term safety:** Data >12 weeks limited for most adaptogens
