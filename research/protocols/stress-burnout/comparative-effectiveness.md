# Comparative Effectiveness: Stress-Burnout Herbs

## Direct Comparisons from Bibliography

**Critical Finding (bibliographies/stress-burnout-protocol.yaml:589-596):**
- **No direct head-to-head comparison trials** between major adaptogens found
- Comparisons below are based on separate trials vs. placebo
- Variability in study designs, populations, outcome measures limits comparability

---

## By Primary Outcome

### Cortisol Reduction (Best → Good)

**1. Ashwagandha** (bibliographies/stress-burnout-protocol.yaml:515-522)
- **Most robust evidence** for directly lowering cortisol
- Significantly decreased serum cortisol after 56-60 days (bibliographies/stress-burnout-protocol.yaml:10-16)
- Meta-analysis confirmed effects (bibliographies/stress-burnout-protocol.yaml:18-25)

**2. Holy Basil**
- Hair cortisol significantly lower (long-term HPA dampening) (bibliographies/stress-burnout-protocol.yaml:172-173)
- Salivary cortisol lower after acute stress (p=0.001) (bibliographies/stress-burnout-protocol.yaml:174)

**3. Panax Ginseng**
- Prevents stress-induced cortisol increase (bibliographies/stress-burnout-protocol.yaml:251-253)
- Ginsenoside Rd dampens stress hormone-triggered cortisol (bibliographies/stress-burnout-protocol.yaml:83-84)

**4. Rhodiola**
- Decreases cortisol response to awakening stress (bibliographies/stress-burnout-protocol.yaml:117-118)

**Ranking:** Ashwagandha > Holy Basil > Panax Ginseng > Rhodiola

### Anxiety Relief (Calming Effect)

**1. Ashwagandha** (bibliographies/stress-burnout-protocol.yaml:524-527)
- **Significantly outperforms Rhodiola** for anxiety reduction (bibliographies/stress-burnout-protocol.yaml:148-150)
- Remarkable improvements in anxiety scores (bibliographies/stress-burnout-protocol.yaml:18-25)
- **Distinctly calming effect** within days to weeks (bibliographies/stress-burnout-protocol.yaml:150)

**2. Holy Basil**
- Lower subjective stress ratings after acute stress (p<0.001) (bibliographies/stress-burnout-protocol.yaml:176-177)
- Improved Perceived Stress Scale scores (p=0.003) (bibliographies/stress-burnout-protocol.yaml:177)
- **Balanced** (not overly sedating)

**3. Bacopa**
- Improved mood and decreased cortisol after **1 dose** (bibliographies/stress-burnout-protocol.yaml:271-272)
- Combined anxiety scores decreased vs. placebo (bibliographies/stress-burnout-protocol.yaml:283-284)

**4. Reishi**
- Sedative effects, reduces depression (bibliographies/stress-burnout-protocol.yaml:321-322)
- Promotes sleep via serotonin pathway (bibliographies/stress-burnout-protocol.yaml:337-339)

**Ranking:** Ashwagandha > Holy Basil > Bacopa > Reishi

## Ashwagandha vs. Rhodiola: The Key Distinction

**Source:** bibliographies/stress-burnout-protocol.yaml:152-160

**Study:** "Combatting stress: Efficacy and safety of Rhodiola rosea and Ashwagandha reviewed" (2024)

**Key Finding:**
> "Overall evidence indicates both have effective anti-stress activity with **different mechanisms**. Rhodiola has more of an **energising effect** while Ashwagandha has **sleep-promoting effects**."

**Clinical Implication:**
- **Ashwagandha**: Choose for anxiety-dominant stress with sleep issues (calming)
- **Rhodiola**: Choose for fatigue-dominant stress with performance demands (energizing)

**Comparative Cortisol:**
- Ashwagandha **directly lowers** cortisol (bibliographies/stress-burnout-protocol.yaml:146-150)
- Rhodiola **decreases cortisol response** to stress (bibliographies/stress-burnout-protocol.yaml:117-118)

**Comparative Anxiety:**
- Ashwagandha **significantly outperforms** Rhodiola for anxiety reduction (bibliographies/stress-burnout-protocol.yaml:148-149)

## Symptom-Specific Matching

### High Anxiety + Sleep Issues
**Choose:** Ashwagandha (first-line)
**Add:** Holy Basil or Reishi for additional sleep support

### Mental Fatigue + Performance Demands
**Choose:** Rhodiola (first-line)
**Add:** Bacopa for memory, or Panax Ginseng for concentration

### Chronic Fatigue Syndrome
**Choose:** Eleuthero (first-line based on RCT evidence)
**Add:** Rhodiola for mental performance
**Consider:** TCM formula (Sijunzi Decoction) - bibliographies/stress-burnout-protocol.yaml:414-422

### Burnout (Exhaustion + Depression + Insomnia)
**Choose:** Rhodiola (strong evidence from German study - bibliographies/stress-burnout-protocol.yaml:488-493)
**Add:** Ashwagandha if anxiety is prominent

### Need for Both Calm AND Focus
**Choose:** Holy Basil (balanced effect)
**Or Combine:** Rhodiola + Ashwagandha

### Low Cortisol States
**Consider:** Licorice (under professional supervision)
**Mechanism:** Cortisol-sparing (slows breakdown vs. increasing production)
**Warning:** Can cause BP/electrolyte issues (bibliographies/stress-burnout-protocol.yaml:576-580)

## Evidence Quality Summary

**No head-to-head trials exist** (bibliographies/stress-burnout-protocol.yaml:591)

**Comparative statements are based on:**
1. Separate placebo-controlled trials
2. Different populations, doses, durations
3. Different outcome measures
4. Systematic reviews attempting synthesis

**Strongest comparative data exists for:**
- Ashwagandha vs. Rhodiola (anxiety, energy effects)
- Combination studies (Rhodiola + Schisandra, etc.)

**Most needed research:**
- Direct head-to-head RCTs (Herb A vs. Herb B vs. Placebo)
- Standardized outcome measures across studies
- Optimal combination ratios for multi-herb formulas
