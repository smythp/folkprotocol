## Key Findings at a Glance

### Top 3 for Acute Treatment:
1. **Peppermint 10% topical** - Fastest (immediate-15 min)
2. **Ginger** - Strongest evidence (p<0.00001)
3. **Lavender inhalation** - 15 minutes, pleasant

### Top 3 for Prevention:
1. **CoQ10 400mg** - NNT=3, excellent safety
2. **Nano-curcumin + Omega-3 + CoQ10** - SMD 1.19 (strongest combination)
3. **Magnesium + Riboflavin + CoQ10** - Complete mitochondrial support

### Most Effective (With Caveat):
- **Butterbur 75mg bid** - 48% reduction BUT hepatotoxicity risk, AAN withdrew 2015

### Best Safety Profile:
- Ginger, Peppermint, Lavender, CoQ10, Magnesium - No significant concerns

### Key Principle:
**Prevention requires 2-4 months minimum**. Don't judge efficacy before then.
